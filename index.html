  • There are two players, Player 1 is on the left and Player 2 is on the right. The current player is designated by a red dot.
  • During each turn, the current player can ROLL two dice as many times as he/she wishes. The dice rolls are added to the player's round score.
  • HOWEVER, if the player rolls a 1, his/her's round score is deleted. The round is then passed to the next player.
  • ALSO, if the player rolls two 6's, then his/her's global score is reset to 0.
  • The player can choose to HOLD the round score, which then gets added to the player's global score. The round is then passed to the next player.
  • The first player to reach the WINNING SCORE is victorious.
  • The WINNING SCORE can be changed on the top left.
